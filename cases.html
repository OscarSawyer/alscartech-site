Kampala • Retail

POS + Inventory for a Growing Supermarket

Challenge: A growing supermarket was relying on manual books and spreadsheets, which made stock control difficult and reduced visibility into daily sales and profit performance.

Our approach: We deployed a cloud-connected POS setup with stock tracking, role-based access, branch-level visibility, and simple training for front-line staff.

Result: Faster checkout, more accurate daily reporting, and better visibility for the owner without needing constant phone calls to the branch.

“We can finally see what’s happening in the business without calling every branch.”

Uganda • Professional Services

CRM Setup for Better Follow-Up and Pipeline Control

Challenge: Leads were coming in through WhatsApp, email, and referrals, but there was no structured process for tracking conversations, follow-ups, or deal stages.

Our approach: We implemented a simple CRM structure with clear pipeline stages, follow-up reminders, and a cleaner way for the team to manage lead status.

Result: Improved visibility into the sales pipeline, better follow-up discipline, and more confidence in how opportunities were being managed.

East Africa • E-commerce

Mobile-First Website Optimization for Online Orders

Challenge: An online shop serving customers in Uganda and Kenya had a slow, heavy website that loaded poorly on mobile data and hurt the buying experience.

Our approach: We rebuilt the site with a lighter mobile-first structure, cleaner page flow, faster-loading media, and SEO aligned with local search behavior.

Result: Better mobile usability, improved loading performance, and a clearer path from product browsing to order completion.
